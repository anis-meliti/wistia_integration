Designed for you, Wistia for Marketing makes your job easier

Create projects and easily move your videos around in your account to suit your organizational structure.

Password protect your videos, require an email to watch, or restrict specific domains.

Generate more awareness and automatically publish your videos directly to your favorite social media platforms.

Optimize your videos for search, and inject metadata into your webpage’s header, without any manual work.

Set your videos to autoplay, change the play button size and visibility, and adjust other video controls with just a few clicks.

Everything a player should be (and more)

Fast loading times

At 99KB, our HTML5 player is fast, and less than a quarter the size of your average video player.

Works on any device

Our responsive player scales to fit any screen size, so whether you're on desktop or mobile, your customizations will display beautifully.

Built with accessibility in mind

Compatible with screen readers, our player has Interactive Captions to make your videos even more inclusive.

High-quality streaming

Our servers instantly measure each viewer’s connection to deliver the best quality video, every time.

Interactive chapters

Interactive chapters give your audience a way to navigate to the parts of your video they care about most.
